About Us

Nestled on the outskirts of South Gurgaon, JSR Green offers farmland that strikes a rare balance between rural serenity and urban access. Buyers here get expansive open plots of freehold land, ideal for weekend retreats, organic farming, or building private farmhouses.

The setting is often characterized by lush greenery, open skies, and relatively low-density surroundings, allowing for clean air, privacy, and a slower pace of life. Yet it doesn't feel completely disconnected — main roads, essential amenities, and city conveniences are typically within a manageable drive.
